Raise Your Helping Hand
Aid Lepers, Inc. is a nonprofit organization that raises money to help eradicate Leprosy and improve the overall quality of life using an integrated (holistic approach to provide for the physical, spiritual and medical needs of men, women, youth and children disadvantaged in Maryland, USA and Ghana, West Africa. The organization was super headed by Dr. “Mrs.” Faustina Quist in 2023 with a vision to raise money to fund and increase awareness in focus areas of healthcare and wellness, leprosy prevention and eradication through research support as well as medical supplies and food to support targeted leprosarium.

Team Profile

DR. FAUSTINA QUIST

DIRECTOR & PRESIDENT
Dr. Faustina Quist, a retired educationist, is the founder and President of Aid Lepers, Inc., a non-profit organization working to eradicate leprosy and improve the quality of life for those affected in Maryland (USA) and Ghana (West Africa). With extensive experience in education, including roles as a school administrator and active involvement in the Ghanaian community, she has made significant contributions to education, youth engagement, and community development.

MR. VINCENT H. KOSI

DIRECTOR
Vincent Hamenu Kosi, a graduate of the University of Cape Coast, resides in Maryland, USA. He currently serves as the president of the Ewe Association of Washington DC Metro Inc. and represents the organization on the Council of Ewe Associations of North America (CEANA). In 1987, as the president of Holy Family Students' Union, he led a visit to the Leprosarium at Weija, making presentations and spending time with the residents under the auspices of Rev Fr. Campbell.

MR. SELORM ANKAH

TREASURER
Selorm Ankah is a finance professional currently residing in Seattle, Washington. He holds an MBA from Youngstown State University and a BSc from Claflin University. He is involved in various charitable organizations within the Greater Seattle area and enjoys mentoring and coaching people in his spare time. .

Mrs. Ninnette Dotsey Adzimahe

Vice President
Mrs. Ninnette Dotsey Adzimahe, Vice President of Aid Lepers Inc., is a dedicated registered Nurse Midwife specializing in high-risk pregnancy at Sinai Hospital, Baltimore. As CEO of ActionCare Health Services, she oversees level 3 home health services. Beyond her medical career, she actively supports her twin teenage children, aids her parents, and volunteers for the Ewe Association in Washington DC Metro area, serving as Vice President for CEANA Nurses Association.

Dr. Faustina Quist

CEO
Dr. Faustina Quist, a retired educationist, is the founder and President of Aid Lepers, Inc., aiming to eradicate leprosy and enhance the quality of life in Maryland and Ghana. As the CEO of Quist Educational Consultancy Inc., she provides consultative services for individuals with special needs. Actively involved in community service, Dr. Quist has contributed to education, served as the President of the Ewe Association of Washington DC Metro Area, and mentors over 30 students.
