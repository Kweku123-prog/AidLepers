Eradicate leprosy, improve lives through. Charity Service
Aid Lepers, Inc. is a nonprofit organization that raises money to help eradicate Leprosy and improve the overall quality of life using an integrated (holistic approach to provide for the physical, spiritual and medical needs of men, women, youth and children disadvantaged in Maryland, USA and Ghana, West Africa. The organization was super headed by Dr. “Mrs.” Faustina Quist in 2023 with a vision to raise money to fund and increase awareness in focus areas of healthcare and wellness, leprosy prevention and eradication through research support as well as medical supplies and food to support targeted leprosarium.
